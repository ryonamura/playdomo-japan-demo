Assumptions & Notes

Important: This estimate is based on information available as of February 2026 and is approximate. Actual costs may vary significantly depending on negotiations, regulatory environment, and market response.
  • Exchange rate assumption: EUR/JPY = ¥160 (used for French attorney fee calculations)
  • Personnel costs: All figures are on a contractor basis. If employees are hired directly, add 15–20% for social insurance contributions, etc.
  • Experience prize cost of goods rate: Assumed at 65%. In practice, the range is 50–75% depending on partner negotiations.
  • Prepaid payment instrument issuance deposit: The statutory deposit amount equals 50% of unused balances. This is highly dependent on user scale and Cosmos token balances; the estimate assumes minimum scale.
  • IP licensing costs: Phase 3 IP collaboration costs can vary significantly based on negotiations (ranging from a few million to hundreds of millions of yen). This estimate assumes an initial small-scale collaboration.
  • Deposit liquidity: The prepaid payment instrument issuance deposit is returned upon business withdrawal, but is locked capital during operations. Note this in cash flow planning.
  • Consumption tax: Development and legal fees are shown excluding tax. A 10% consumption tax applies in practice.
  • Withdrawal costs at Go/No-Go: If a No-Go decision is made in Phase 0, no additional withdrawal costs are required. A No-Go decision after Phase 1 may incur additional costs.
